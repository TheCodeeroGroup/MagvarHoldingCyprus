About Magvar Holding Limited

Magvar Holding Limited is a full-service property investment company that helps high-net-worth investors add the strength of global real estate to their portfolio.

Our unique end-to-end approach, delivered by an experienced team of international real estate and investment professionals, untangles the complexities and complications of property investment. Our end goal is for investors and international landlords to be able to manage and capitalise on their property investments just as easily as they would any other globally recognised asset class.

We pride ourselves on the long-term relationships we build with preferred developers, to whom we offer strong financial commitment and overseas marketing support in return for priority access to the best global property investment opportunities for our clients. These opportunities are then put through a rigorous process of due diligence before being selected for presentation to investors and distributors. We provide comprehensive support throughout every stage of the investment process including, if required, the ongoing management, rental and resale of investments.

To date, the value of the property our clients have invested in through us is over 100 mln EUR across 5 markets worldwide. Our target investments are EU countries, especially strong in Austria and Germany. At every step of the way, we deliver consummate credibility, extensive expertise and a proven partnership approach.
